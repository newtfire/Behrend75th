Letter from Mary and Ernst Behrend to Warren Behrend, 1929-12-17 pg1


1929-12-17
Dear Warren-


Headed in
Southern Pines. Next day
Charleston & the Amida
hill ? wonderful to see
you & H. & all to
& gather for Christmas.
We telephoned Betty &
offically invite her -and
she says she is coming
lawnell 27.?. Possibly
You have heard were
about this. Much love
think of how much
we care for you and
