March 31, 1909.
Wednesday 4.30 AM. "An early spring."
This is you springing out
of bed, ready to murder
the alarm clock!

Mary has drawn a picture of this bedroom scene and once again uses a pun. She says "an early spring". This both referencing the person in the image getting out of bed early, and the season of spring coming early.—#lhering
