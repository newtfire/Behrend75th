April 14, 1909.
Wednesday Love from Mary Alicia M.B

In the picture the woman sitting down is most likely Mary Behrend because her initials are written at the bottom of the page right next to the lady. Then the woman standing up is most likey one of her servants/maids. The infant is probably one of her children and most likely Warren because he was born around 1909—#lhering
