April 6, 1909.
Tuesday
Theresa makes us every day
lovely puddings & pies
sometimes I order them
sometimes they're a surprise! Don't eat the really sweet things!!

It is apparent that Theresa and Mary are probably friends because sometimes she gives Mary desserts as a surprise. It is also possible that Theresa is a baker and owns a bakery.—#lhering
