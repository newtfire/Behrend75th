May 12, 1909.
Wednesday
"There's something queer inside that thing"
Gamie seems to think-
"I really don't know what it is
That can only sleep and drink"
What do you think is inside!

This shows that Gamie is not sure about the baby that is inside of the stroller, and is very confused about the baby.—zmurphy
